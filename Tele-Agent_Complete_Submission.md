## 2. Description
Tele-Agent seamlessly mirrors your Telegram messages between your iPhone and Apple Watch with real-time sync, encrypted relay, smart filtering, and intuitive quick-reply options. 

Designed for professionals, traders, and developers who can't afford to miss a beat, Tele-Agent brings the power of Telegram to your wrist without the clutter. Our advanced relay technology ensures that your messages arrive instantly and securely, with end-to-end encryption preserving your privacy.

**Key Features:**
- **Real-Time Mirroring**: Instant sync between iPhone and Apple Watch.
- **Secure Relay**: Fully encrypted data transmission.
- **Quick Replies**: Respond via voice, text, or scribble.
- **Smart Filtering**: Prioritize important contacts and mute the noise.
- **Haptic Feedback**: Subtle notifications that keep you informed without distraction.
- **Multi-Device Support**: Works harmoniously across your Apple ecosystem.
