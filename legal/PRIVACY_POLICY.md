# Privacy Policy for Tele-Agent

**Last Updated: April 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Tele-Agent ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Telegram Messages**: We relay your Telegram messages between your iPhone and Apple Watch
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version for troubleshooting
- **Usage Analytics**: Features used, errors encountered (optional)

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **IP Address**: For relay server authentication
- **Device Identifiers**: To sync between iPhone and Watch
- **Crash Logs**: For app stability improvements

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Relay messages between your iPhone and Apple Watch
- Improve app performance and stability
- Debug technical issues
- Provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

**We do NOT:**
- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Store your messages permanently
- Use your data for advertising
- Share with Telegram beyond what Telegram API requires

## 4. End-to-End Encryption

- Messages are encrypted during relay
- Only your devices can decrypt messages
- Encryption keys never transmitted unencrypted
- Our servers cannot read your messages

## 5. Data Retention

- Messages: Deleted after delivery (max 24 hours)
- Logs: Retained for 7 days maximum
- Device tokens: Retained while app installed
- No indefinite backups of personal messages

## 6. Third-Party Services

### Telegram
- We use official Telegram Bot API
- Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy
- Your chat history controlled by Telegram
- We don't have access to your Telegram password

### Analytics (Optional)
- Crash reporting: Firebase Crashlytics (Google)
- Anonymized, no PII sent
- You can opt-out in settings

## 7. Data Security

- **TLS 1.3** encryption for all network traffic
- **AES-256** encryption for message relay
- Secure token storage using iOS/watchOS Keychain
- Regular security audits
- No plaintext storage of sensitive data

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (submit request)
- **Delete** your account and data
- **Export** your message history
- **Opt-out** of analytics
- **Withdraw** consent anytime

Submit requests to: **privacy@teleagent.app**

## 9. Children's Privacy

Tele-Agent is not intended for children under 13 (16 in EU). We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used our app, contact us immediately.

## 10. International Data Transfers

If accessing from EU:
- Data processed under GDPR
- Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers
- Your data processed only in EU, US
- Right to lodge complaint with DPA

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have right to:
- Know what data is collected
- Delete personal information
- Opt-out of sale/sharing
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

Submit requests at: **privacy@teleagent.app**

## 14. Additional Information

### Message Content
- Stored temporarily during relay only
- Deleted from our servers after delivery
- Not indexed, searchable, or archived by us
- Subject to Telegram's message retention
